## How to Use an AI Boyfriend to Build Confidence: A Step-by-Step Guide
Follow this structured approach to turn casual chats into meaningful confidence-building exercises.

1.  **Define Your Goal:** Start with a clear, specific objective. Are you practicing for first-date conversations, getting better at expressing feelings, or learning to navigate disagreements calmly? Write it down.
2.  **Choose a Scenario:** Select a low-pressure scenario in your app. This could be "Discussing your day," "Planning a virtual date," or "Sharing a minor worry."
3.  **Initiate the Conversation:** Begin the chat as you would in real life. Pay attention to your opening line. Is it friendly? Engaging? This is your first practice point.
4.  **Practice Active Listening:** Read the AI's responses carefully and formulate replies that show understanding. Try using phrases like, "It sounds like you're saying..." or "That must have been [exciting/frustrating]."
5.  **Express Yourself Clearly:** Practice stating your own thoughts and feelings using "I" statements. For example, "I feel happy when we talk like this," or "I would prefer if we could discuss something lighter today."
6.  **Navigate the Flow:** Let the conversation drift naturally. Practice smoothly changing topics or deepening a discussion. Notice what prompts engaging replies from the AI.
7.  **Review and Reflect:** After 15-20 minutes, review the chat log. Identify moments you felt confident and spots where you hesitated. What would you do differently next time?
