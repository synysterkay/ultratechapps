## 3 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1.  **Ignoring the 'Why' Behind the Probability:** Don't just look at the win percentage. The real value is in the match insights and team analytics. A 60% probability based on strong defensive form is different from a 60% probability based on a star player's return. Always dig into the supporting data.
2.  **Letting Bias Override the Data:** This is the biggest pitfall. You might be a loyal fan of Team A, but the AI shows a 70% probability for Team B. Trust the process you've built. The AI has no emotional attachment, and neither should your analysis.
3.  **Chasing Losses or Doubting the System:** After an incorrect prediction, some users abandon the tool or make impulsive, emotion-driven picks on the next match. Stick to your weekly routine. AI predictions are about long-term accuracy, not winning every single call.
