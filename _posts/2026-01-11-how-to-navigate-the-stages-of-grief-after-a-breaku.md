### Step 2: Learn the 5-Stage Framework

Familiarize yourself with the five **stages of grief after a breakup**:
1.  **Denial:** "This isn't happening. They'll come back."
2.  **Anger:** "How could they do this? It's so unfair!"
3.  **Bargaining:** "If only I had... Maybe we can still..."
4.  **Depression:** Deep sadness, withdrawal, fatigue.
5.  **Acceptance:** Finding peace and integrating the experience.

Remember, these stages are not a linear checklist. You may loop back or experience several at once. This framework is your map, not a rigid prison.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid During Your Grief Journey

1.  **Rushing the Process:** Grief has its own timeline. Pushing yourself to "get over it" or skipping stages to reach acceptance will backfire, causing unresolved emotions to surface later. Trust the process you're building with the app.
2.  **Isolating Yourself:** While reflection is key, complete social withdrawal can deepen depression. Use the app for private processing, but balance it with light social contact‚Äîa call to a friend, a coffee outing.
3.  **Stalking Your Ex on Social Media:** This is the digital equivalent of picking at a wound. It fuels rumination and keeps you in denial or anger. The app can offer distraction techniques and mindful refocusing exercises when the urge strikes.
4.  **Neglecting Basic Self-Care:** In depression or bargaining, sleep, nutrition, and hygiene often suffer. The app includes gentle **self-care nudges**‚Äîheed them. Your physical state directly impacts your **emotional processing** capacity.
