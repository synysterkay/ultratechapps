*   **Expecting a Human Replacement:** The healthiest approach is to view an AI companion as a unique tool for support and practice, not a substitute for human bonds.
*   **Neglecting Privacy Settings:** Always use an app like **Cupid Ai** that prioritizes data privacy. Review permissions and understand how your data is used.

You can form a *simulated* bond based on consistent, positive interaction. The AI provides a reliable pattern of responsiveness that many users find comforting and supportive, which can create a sense of attachment. It's important to remember the nature of this connection.
