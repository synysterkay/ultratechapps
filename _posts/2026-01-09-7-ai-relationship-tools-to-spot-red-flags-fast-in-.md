## Common Mistakes to Avoid When Scanning for Red Flags
While **AI relationship tools** are powerful, their effectiveness depends on how you use them.

*   **Over-Reliance on AI:** The tool is an advisor, not an oracle. Your personal judgment and feelings are paramount. Use it for perspective, not to make decisions for you.
*   **Ignoring Context:** AI analyzes the data you provide. It may not understand inside jokes or deeply personal contexts. Always apply human understanding to the results.
*   **Using It as a Weapon:** The goal is personal insight and health, not to "catch" or accuse a partner. The insights are for your reflection first.
*   **Scanning Every Single Message:** This leads to anxiety. Use the tool periodically when you sense a pattern or feel uncertain, not for daily surveillance.
*   **Dismissing Your Gut Because AI Didn't Flag It:** If something feels wrong, it is worth exploring, even if an AI scan doesn't highlight it. Technology complements intuition; it doesn't replace it.
