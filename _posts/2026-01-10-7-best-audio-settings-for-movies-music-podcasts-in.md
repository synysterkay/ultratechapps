## Common Audio Configuration Mistakes to Avoid

*   **Using the Same EQ for Everything:** The "Bass Boost" setting that makes music punchy can drown out podcast dialogue.
*   **Maxing Out All Sliders:** Pushing every frequency and boost to 100% causes distortion, clipping, and can damage speakers over time.
*   **Ignoring Headphone vs. Speaker Profiles:** Speakers and headphones have different acoustic properties. Always use the correct output profile.
*   **Forgetting to Save Profiles:** Manually reconfiguring settings for movies, music, and podcasts is inefficient. Save them as presets.
*   **Over-relying on System Volume:** Android and iOS have built-in limits. A dedicated **volume booster app for Android** safely amplifies signal beyond these caps.

## Expert Tips for Music Audio Perfection
