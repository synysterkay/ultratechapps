    <p>This Cookie Policy explains how Best Ai Apps ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile applications. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.</p>

    <h2>1. What Are Cookies?</h2>
    <p>Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit websites or use apps. They help us recognize your device and remember information about your visit.</p>

    <h3>Types of Technologies We Use:</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Cookies:</strong> Text files stored in your browser</li>
      <li><strong>Local Storage:</strong> Data stored in your device's storage</li>
      <li><strong>Session Storage:</strong> Temporary data cleared when you close the app/browser</li>
      <li><strong>Mobile Identifiers:</strong> Device IDs for mobile apps (IDFA, AAID)</li>
      <li><strong>Pixels/Beacons:</strong> Invisible images that track interactions</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>2. Why We Use Cookies</h2>
    <p>We use cookies and similar technologies for several purposes:</p>

    <h3>2.1 Essential/Functional</h3>
    <p>These are necessary for our services to function properly:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Authentication (keeping you logged in)</li>
      <li>Security (preventing fraud and abuse)</li>
      <li>Session management (maintaining your settings)</li>
      <li>Load balancing (distributing traffic)</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Legal Basis:</strong> Necessary for service delivery (no consent required)</p>

    <h3>2.2 Performance/Analytics</h3>
    <p>These help us understand how users interact with our services:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Page views and navigation patterns</li>
      <li>Feature usage and engagement metrics</li>
      <li>Error tracking and crash reporting</li>
      <li>Performance optimization</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Examples:</strong> Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics</p>
    <p><strong>Legal Basis:</strong> Legitimate interest (consent may be required in some regions)</p>

    <h3>2.3 Functional/Preference</h3>
    <p>These remember your choices and personalize your experience:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Language preferences</li>
      <li>Theme settings (light/dark mode)</li>
      <li>Saved filters or display options</li>
      <li>Notification preferences</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Legal Basis:</strong> Legitimate interest or consent</p>

    <h3>2.4 Marketing/Advertising</h3>
    <p>Currently, we do NOT use third-party advertising cookies. If we implement advertising in the future, we will:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Update this policy with detailed information</li>
      <li>Obtain explicit consent where required</li>
      <li>Provide opt-out mechanisms</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>5. How to Control Cookies</h2>

    <h3>5.1 Browser Settings</h3>
    <p>Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Chrome:</strong> Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies</li>
      <li><strong>Safari:</strong> Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data</li>
      <li><strong>Firefox:</strong> Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data</li>
      <li><strong>Edge:</strong> Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>5.2 Mobile Devices</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>iOS:</strong> Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Limit Ad Tracking</li>
      <li><strong>Android:</strong> Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>5.3 Opt-Out Tools</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Google Analytics:</strong> <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Browser Add-on</a></li>
      <li><strong>Network Advertising Initiative:</strong> <a href="https://optout.networkadvertising.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Opt-Out Tool</a></li>
      <li><strong>Digital Advertising Alliance:</strong> <a href="https://optout.aboutads.info/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Consumer Choice</a></li>
    </ul>

    <h2>6. Impact of Disabling Cookies</h2>
    <p>If you disable or refuse cookies:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>✅ <strong>Still Works:</strong> Basic app functionality and core features</li>
      <li>❌ <strong>May Not Work:</strong> Personalization, saved preferences, analytics</li>
      <li>❌ <strong>Won't Work:</strong> "Remember Me" login, session persistence</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.</p>

    <h2>7. Do Not Track (DNT)</h2>
    <p>Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not alter our practices based on DNT signals, but you can still control cookies through your browser settings.</p>

    <h2>8. Data Retention</h2>
    <p>We retain cookie data as follows:</p>
    <ul>

      <li><strong>Persistent Cookies:</strong> Stored for the duration specified (e.g., 1 year)</li>
      <li><strong>Analytics Data:</strong> Aggregated and anonymized after 14-26 months</li>
